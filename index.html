IEEE HMRITM Timeline

Branch Opening Year

April 2009, With the excitement to learn more from IEEE, HMRITM opened its IEEE Student Branch with a Grand Opening.

Branch Re-Opening

March 2017, Under the Supervision of Dr. Anuradha Tomar, IEEE HMRITM was reopened with target to stablize and learn more from networking.

Presenting IEEE HMRITM Student Branch

January 2019, Under the leadership of our Chair Ramneek Kalra, IEEE HMRITM re-discovered its soul and Family Members for the trust of spreading IEEE across Country!
